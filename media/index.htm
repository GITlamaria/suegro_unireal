OUR WORLD ENTERS A NEW UNIVERSAL REALITY AT UNIREAL.COM

MONTREAL, CANADA, December 22, 1999 -- As the millennium closes humanity finds itself at the center of a sudden thought-quake: an idea is put forth that will rock the foundation on which our world and belief systems had relied since time immemorial.

The unification of two pillars of Physics in the twentieth century, the Relativity theory of Albert Einstein and Quantum Mechanics, developed and elaborated by many renowned scientists from Max Planck early in the century to current Physics laureate Gerard ‘t Hooft, has been pursued for the better part of this century but seemed unreachable.  Despite the efforts of great minds from Erwin Schrödinger to Stephen Hawking and billions of dollars spent on particle accelerators and neutrino observatories, the search for the elusive fundamental nature of the universe remained unfulfilled.  Now this grand goal has been achieved by expanding Relativity in a way that remains consistent with this theory; the world wide web (WWW) has been chosen as the forum for presenting its proof.

Supported by illustrative animations and diagrams provided by Anthony Leblanc (Graphic Design) and Eric S. Kruller (Additional Graphics) under the supervision of Sandeep Panesar (PR and Website Coordinator), Unireal.com promises to deliver a new reality, that will affect and change us all.

The launching of this web site will let us enter into the new millennium with a fresh perspective to meet its new challenges. Visit www.unireal.com for the future.
